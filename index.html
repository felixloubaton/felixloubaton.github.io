I'm currently a Postdoc at the MPIM under the supervision of Viktoriya Ozornova. I'm interested in higher categories, both strict and homotopic versions. Before that, I was a doctoral student at the Université de Nice Côte d'Azur, in the Laboratoire Jean Alexandre Dieudonné, under the supervision of Carlos Simpson and Denis-Charles Cisinski.
